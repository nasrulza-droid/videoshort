Participant Requirements

  • Open for active high school students (or equivalent) worldwide.
  • Individual or team of up to 3 students from the same school.
  • Each student may only join 1 team.
  • Each team may submit only 1 video entry.
  • Entry must be original and must not have won other competitions.

Video Requirements

  • Duration 1-3 minutes, landscape orientation, mp4 format.
  • Minimum resolution 720p (HD).
  • Language: Indonesian or English.
  • IPDCEC logo and school logo must be visible at top-right throughout the video.
  • Must include title, problem background, solution, impact, and references (if any).
  • Audio/visual assets must be royalty-free or properly credited.
  • AI use is allowed, but AI tools must be disclosed in description or end credits.

FAQ

Is AI mandatory for video production?

No. AI is optional. If used, mention the AI tools in the description or end credits.

Must Instagram account stay public?

Yes. Your account must remain public during judging until winner announcement.

Can we submit a previously awarded video?

No. The entry must be original and must not have won in other competitions.
